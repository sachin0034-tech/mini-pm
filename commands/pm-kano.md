## Kano Model Categories

| Category | If present | If absent | Strategy |
|----------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Basic (Must-Be)** | Neutral | Extreme dissatisfaction | Fix parity gaps first |
| **Performance (Linear)** | More = more satisfied | Less = less satisfied | Invest proportionally |
| **Delight (Attractive)** | Delight | No dissatisfaction | Surprise & differentiate |
| **Indifferent** | Doesn't matter | Doesn't matter | Deprioritize |
| **Reverse** | Causes dissatisfaction | Causes satisfaction | Remove or make optional |

## Kano Survey Design (for each feature)

Ask two questions per feature:
1. **Functional:** "If [feature] IS available, how do you feel?" 
2. **Dysfunctional:** "If [feature] is NOT available, how do you feel?"

Scale: Delighted / Expect it / Neutral / Live with it / Dislike it
