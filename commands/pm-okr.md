## 6. Scoring Guide
- **0.0–0.3:** Did not attempt or major blocker hit
- **0.4–0.6:** Made progress but missed materially
- **0.7–0.9:** Hit the target (this is SUCCESS — not a failure)
- **1.0:** Exceptional — revisit whether targets were ambitious enough
