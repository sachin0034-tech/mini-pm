## Portfolio Presentation Tips
- Lead with the outcome in the title: "[Product] → [Result]" not "[Product] redesign"
- Include 3–5 artifacts: mockups, data charts, decision memos, before/after
- Keep to 5–7 slides or 800 words max — recruiters skim
- Your job is to show thinking, not just results
- Weakness reflection = seniority signal
