## 3. Behavioral Question Bank (with PM-specific focus)

### Strategy & Vision
- "What's your product philosophy?"
- "Tell me about a time you shaped a multi-year product strategy."
- "How do you know when a product is ready to launch?"

### Data & Metrics
- "How do you measure success for a product you shipped?"
- "Tell me about a time data changed your mind."
- "Design a metrics framework for [product type]."

### Prioritization
- "Tell me about a hard prioritization decision with competing stakeholders."
- "How do you say no to a feature request from leadership?"
- "Walk me through how you built a roadmap."

### Cross-Functional Leadership
- "Tell me about a time you influenced eng / design / data without authority."
- "Describe a disagreement with an engineer and how you resolved it."
- "How do you build trust with technical teams?"

### Failure & Learning
- "Tell me about a product that failed or underperformed."
- "What's a PM mistake you've made and what did you learn?"

### AI/ML-Specific (if relevant)
- "How do you evaluate the quality of an AI feature?"
- "How do you build a product roadmap when model capabilities are uncertain?"
- "Tell me about a time you had to balance AI quality vs. latency vs. cost."

## 4. Product Design Questions (with answer framework)

**CIRCLES Method for product design:**
- C — Customers (who are they?)
- I — Identify needs
- R — Report constraints
- C — Cut scope / prioritize
- L — List solutions
- E — Evaluate trade-offs
- S — Summarize with recommendation

**Sample questions to practice:**
- "Design a PM tool for first-time PMs."
- "Improve [product] for enterprise customers."
- "Design an AI assistant for [domain]."

---

## 5. Case Interview Framework

For estimation questions:
1. State your approach before calculating
2. Break into known components
3. Apply reasonable assumptions (state them explicitly)
4. Sanity-check your answer against real-world intuition

For metrics questions:
1. Clarify the goal (growth / engagement / retention / revenue?)
2. Define the north star metric
3. Build a metric tree
4. Identify leading indicators
5. Design guardrail metrics

---

## 6. Questions to Ask the Interviewer

### About the Role
- "What does success look like in the first 90 days?"
- "What's the hardest part of this role right now?"
- "What would a top performer in this role do differently from an average performer?"

### About the Team
- "How does PM work with engineering and design here?"
- "What's the PM-to-engineer ratio?"

### About the Company
- "What's the biggest strategic bet the company is making in the next 18 months?"
- "Where do you see the most uncertainty in the company's strategy?"
