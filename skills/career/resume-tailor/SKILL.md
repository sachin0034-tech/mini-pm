## 4. Resume Bullet Formula

The strongest PM bullets follow this structure:
> **[Action verb at seniority level]** + **[what you did]** + **[scope/scale]** + **[quantified result]** + **[business context why it mattered]**

**Strong action verbs for senior PMs:**
- Defined, shaped, drove, launched, scaled, rebuilt, orchestrated, championed
- **Avoid:** worked on, helped with, contributed to, collaborated on
