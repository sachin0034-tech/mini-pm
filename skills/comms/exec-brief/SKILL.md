## TL;DR (3 sentences max)
State: what's happening, why it matters, what you're asking for.
A busy exec should be able to stop reading here and still know what they need to decide.

---

## Situation (2–3 sentences)
What is the current state? What changed or what new information exists?
No jargon. No history lesson. Only what's needed to understand the decision.
