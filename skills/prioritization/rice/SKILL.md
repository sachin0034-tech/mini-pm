## RICE Framework Reference
**RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort**

| Factor | Definition | Scale |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Reach** | # of users/customers affected per quarter | Absolute number |
| **Impact** | Degree of impact on target metric per user | 3=Massive, 2=High, 1=Med, 0.5=Low, 0.25=Minimal |
| **Confidence** | How sure are you of estimates? | 100%=High, 80%=Med, 50%=Low |
| **Effort** | Person-months of work (all roles) | Absolute number |

## What RICE Is NOT Telling You
- Features that have regulatory/compliance requirements (do regardless of score)
- Features that are table-stakes (must-have for market viability)
- Features at risk of competitive loss if not shipped soon
