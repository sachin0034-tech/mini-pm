## Opportunity Scoring Framework

**Formula:** Opportunity Score = Importance + MAX(Importance − Satisfaction, 0)

- **Score > 10:** Dramatically underserved — high-value opportunity
- **Score 8–10:** Underserved — worth pursuing
- **Score < 8:** Competitively served or over-served
