**Category types:**

- **Performance** — it's too slow / unreliable
- **Comprehension** — they don't understand

- **Workflow** — doesn't fit how they work

**Severity scale (1–5):**
- 5: Blocks core use case entirely
- 4: Major friction, forces workaround

- 2: Minor inconvenience
