## 3. Interview Structure (60 min guide)

### Opening (5 min)
- Introduce yourself, explain the session purpose (learning, not selling)
- Ask permission to record
- Set expectations: no wrong answers, you want candid feedback
- "To start, can you tell me a little about your role and what a typical [day/week] looks like?"

### Warm-Up: Their World (10 min)
- "Walk me through the last time you had to [relevant task]."
- "How often does that come up?"
- "Who else is involved when that happens?"
